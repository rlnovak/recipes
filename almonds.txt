Almond tart

Ingredients:

100g sliced almonds
2 tbs. fresh cream
1 tb. honey
200g sugar

For the base:

150g all purpose wheat flour
100g butter "demi sel"
1 egg yolk

Make a basic "pate brisee" with the "base" ingredients

In a small pan, melt the honey, then add the cream, sugar, butter and almonds. Turn off when it boils.
Pour on top of pre-baked (10 minutes) base, and bake for approx. 15 min.

Enjoy!
